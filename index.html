Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Kenwood GPS map updates.

How often should I update my Kenwood GPS maps?

We recommend updating your Kenwood GPS maps at least once a year to ensure you have the latest road information, points of interest, and navigation features. Major road changes and new developments are continuously added to our database. For frequent travelers or those who rely heavily on navigation for daily commuting, updating every 6 months is ideal to stay current with the rapidly changing road infrastructure.

What do I need to install the Kenwood GPS map update?

You'll need a computer with internet access, a USB drive with sufficient storage capacity (typically 32GB or more), and your Kenwood vehicle. The Kenwood GPS map update process typically takes 30-60 minutes to complete in your vehicle. Ensure your vehicle's battery is fully charged or the engine is running during the update process to prevent any interruptions.

Can I use the navigation system while updating my Kenwood GPS maps?

No, the navigation system will be unavailable during the Kenwood GPS map update process. We recommend performing the update when you don't need to use navigation, such as when your vehicle is parked safely at home. The system will display a progress indicator during the update, and once completed, it will automatically restart with the new maps installed.
